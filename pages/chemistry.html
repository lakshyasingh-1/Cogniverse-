Chemistry Timeline

Discover how chemistry evolved through different civilizations and scientific revolutions.

Ancient Alchemy

3000 BCE – 1500 CE

Ancient civilizations experimented with metals, minerals, and medicinal substances. Alchemy aimed to transform matter and discover immortality.

Islamic Chemistry

800 CE – 1400 CE

Jabir ibn Hayyan introduced laboratory experimentation, acids, purification techniques, and scientific observation.

Modern Chemistry Begins

1600 CE – 1800 CE

Robert Boyle and Antoine Lavoisier transformed chemistry into a scientific discipline using measurements and experimentation.

Atomic Theory

1800 CE – 1900 CE

Dalton proposed atomic theory while Mendeleev created the periodic table, organizing chemical elements scientifically.

Modern Molecular Science

1900 CE – Present

Chemistry now explores nanotechnology, pharmaceuticals, biochemistry, polymers, and sustainable energy materials.
