Explore the intellectual journey of philosophy from ancient wisdom and logic to ethics, metaphysics, existentialism, and modern philosophical thought.

Explore Timeline

Philosophy Timeline

Discover how philosophical thought evolved through civilizations, influencing science, politics, ethics, and human understanding.

Ancient Eastern Philosophy

1500 BCE – 500 BCE

Indian and Chinese philosophers explored spirituality, morality, meditation, and the nature of existence through texts and teachings.

Greek Philosophy

600 BCE – 300 BCE

Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle developed logic, ethics, metaphysics, and rational inquiry that shaped Western thought.

Medieval Philosophy

400 CE – 1400 CE

Philosophers connected religion and reason, discussing theology, morality, and the relationship between faith and logic.

Enlightenment Era

1600 CE – 1800 CE

Thinkers emphasized reason, liberty, science, and human rights, influencing politics and modern democratic societies.

Modern Philosophy

1900 CE – Present

Existentialism, phenomenology, analytical philosophy, and postmodernism reshaped discussions on reality and identity.
