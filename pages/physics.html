The Evolution of Physics

Journey through the history of physics from ancient philosophy to quantum mechanics, relativity, and modern cosmology.

Explore Timeline

Physics Timeline

Discover how humanity understood motion, energy, matter, gravity, and the universe across different eras.

Ancient Physics

600 BCE – 300 BCE

Greek philosophers like Aristotle attempted to explain nature, motion, matter, and the cosmos using logic and observation.

Islamic Golden Age

800 CE – 1400 CE

Ibn al-Haytham revolutionized optics and experimental science, contributing to the scientific method and light theory.

Scientific Revolution

1500 CE – 1700 CE

Galileo, Kepler, and Newton transformed physics through mathematics, astronomy, and laws of motion and gravity.

Classical Physics

1700 CE – 1900 CE

Scientists explored thermodynamics, electricity, magnetism, and electromagnetic waves during this era.

Modern Physics

1900 CE – Present

Einstein’s relativity and quantum mechanics reshaped humanity’s understanding of space, time, atoms, and energy.
